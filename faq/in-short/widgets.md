# Widgets

Widgets are blocks that lead to another platform. Some social widgets have a preview of the content, which you can configure through the widget settings.

## ✔️ Available widgets

* Essentials
  * Rich text (markdown)
  * Title
  * Link
* Social media
  * YouTube video
  * Twitter tweet
  * Twitch live stream
  * Instagram post
  * Pinterest pin
* Music
  * SoundCloud track
  * Spotify track
  * Spotify album
  * Spotify playlist
  * Spotify artist
